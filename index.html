Carrboro's Got Talent

Summer 2020

Overview

Carrboro's Got Talent is a low-pressure online talent show for amateurs and for fun. If you are reading this, then you are invited to participate!

Content

Anything that you can do over a webcam is game. Pluck a guitar, or perform stand-up. Deliver a speech, or show off a painting. It's up to you! The stage is yours for up to 10 minutes. There are only 15 spots so consider signing up with the form below.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help motivate you to do something that you already want to do. And we achieve this by providing you with a simple dealine and achievable goal. Just show up and do your best on the day of the show! (TBD, late September)
